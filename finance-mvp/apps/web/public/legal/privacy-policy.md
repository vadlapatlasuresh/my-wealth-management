balances, transactions, and related financial data.

and cookies / local storage / a service worker used to run the app and keep you signed in.

## 4. Security
We use industry-standard safeguards. Sensitive identifiers (full SSN/EIN) and linked-account
access tokens are **encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM)**; only the last four digits of an SSN/EIN
are ever displayed. Access is restricted and audited. Transport is encrypted (HTTPS). No
method is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
